UK Government Expenditure

Public spending is defined as "money spent by the Government on public services and other state-controlled operations, projects and investments.".

The UK public spending expenditure data in this demo has been sourced from the Public Expenditure Statistical Analyses (PESA) 2016 report.

PESA consists of ten Excel spreadsheets, each spreadsheet containing up to eight separate tables. A document which summarizes the data is provided here.
